First Aid Tips

1. CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation)

Perform chest compressions and rescue breaths if someone is unresponsive and not breathing.

2. Choking

If someone is choking, perform the Heimlich maneuver to clear the airway.

3. Burns

For minor burns, cool the affected area with running water. For severe burns, seek medical help.
